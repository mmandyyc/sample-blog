Single O: For the People Who Really Care About Coffee ☕⚡

A lively Surry Hills institution where serious coffee, thoughtful brunch and passionate staff all show up strong.

★★★★★

I finally made it to Single O, and as someone who takes coffee seriously, I was curious to see if it lived up to the hype. Right away, I noticed the energy—busy, lively, and full of people who clearly know their coffee.

I ordered a filter brew to really taste what they’re about, and it didn’t disappoint. It was smooth, slightly fruity, and incredibly clean.

For food, I went with their breakfast plate featuring eggs, greens, and some house-made extras that added a bit of creativity to the dish. Everything felt thoughtful, not just thrown together for the sake of it. The portions were satisfying without being too heavy, which I appreciated.

What I liked most was how the staff seemed genuinely passionate. When I asked about the coffee, they explained it in a way that was informative but not overwhelming. It made the whole experience feel more personal.

The atmosphere is definitely on the buzzier side, so it’s not somewhere I’d go to sit quietly for hours, but it’s great if you want that vibrant café energy. If you care about good coffee and well-executed brunch, Single O is a must-visit.
