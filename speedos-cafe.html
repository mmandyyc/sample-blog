Speedos Café: A Beachside Brunch That’s About More Than the View 🌊🥣

A Bondi-adjacent brunch stop where beach energy, colourful food and easygoing service all come together.

★★★★★

I headed to Speedos Café mainly for the view, but the food ended up being just as memorable. Sitting right by the beach, I could hear the waves as I flipped through the menu, which already put me in a good mood.

I decided to go for something fresh and ordered a smoothie bowl along with their classic brunch plate. The smoothie bowl arrived first—bright, colorful, and loaded with fruit. It was refreshing without being overly sweet, and the textures were great with crunchy granola on top.

For the main, I had eggs with avocado and sourdough, plus a side of smoked salmon. Everything tasted clean and well-balanced, like it hadn’t been overcomplicated.

I lingered over an iced coffee, just watching people walk along the shoreline. That’s really the charm of Speedos—you don’t feel like rushing through your meal. It’s more about soaking in the surroundings while enjoying good, uncomplicated food.

Service was easygoing and friendly, and even though it was busy, nothing felt chaotic. Overall, it’s one of those spots where the experience goes beyond just what’s on the plate. If you want a relaxed beachside brunch with solid food, this place is hard to beat.
